Why Us?


Color Locked is an online platform to support local artists and students, who have converted their hobby and talent to a means of earning. Due to the pandemic, it is not possible for them to showcase their talent in Galleries and exhibitions.


Our website gives them the platform to broadcast their work to a wider public. You can support them by buying and appreciating their art.


You can customize the canvas size according to your needs and we will get it ready and delivered to you.
